About Us

Founded in 1974 by Billay Dhanjal, Dhanjal Motor Works has built a strong reputation for providing high-quality automotive services. With over four decades of experience, we specialize in panel beating, spray painting, servicing, and general overhauls. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has helped us grow into a trusted name in the industry.

We serve a wide range of clients, including individuals, corporate clients, parastatals, and major insurance companies. Whether it's restoring bodywork through expert panel beating, enhancing your vehicle’s appearance with spray painting, ensuring top performance with our thorough servicing, we offer comprehensive solutions tailored to meet each client’s unique needs.

Our team is dedicated to delivering precise, reliable, and efficient services. Using state-of-the-art equipment and techniques, we ensure that every job, big or small, is completed to the highest standards. We take pride in our work and strive to exceed expectations, whether it’s a routine service or a complex repair.

At Dhanjal Motor Works, we understand that each customer has different requirements. That’s why we take a personalized approach to every project, ensuring that we meet the specific needs of individuals, businesses, and insurance partners alike. This customer-focused philosophy has earned us long-lasting relationships and repeat business.

With a legacy of over 50 years, Dhanjal Motor Works continues to uphold the values of integrity, quality, and dedication that our founder set in motion. As we look to the future, we remain committed to delivering outstanding automotive solutions that our clients can rely on.
