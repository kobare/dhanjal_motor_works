Our Services

Expert solutions for panel beating, spray painting, servicing, and hauling needs.

Panel Beating

Our expert panel beating services restore your vehicle’s bodywork to perfection, using advanced techniques to remove dents, scratches, and collision damage with precision for a flawless finish.

Spray Painting

We offer high-quality spray painting services in a professional spray booth to restore your vehicle’s finish. Our team ensures a smooth, flawless result with precise custom color code matching for a like-new appearance.

Vehicle Servicing

Comprehensive vehicle servicing packages that include engine checks, brake inspections, oil changes, and more, keeping your vehicle in optimal condition for long-lasting performance.

Minor and Major Repairs

Whether it's a small fix or a major overhaul, we handle both minor and major repairs to ensure your vehicle is road-ready quickly. Our team works efficiently to address any issue, big or small.

Engine Diagnostics

We use advanced diagnostics tools to identify performance issues with your vehicle’s engine. From misfires to fuel system issues, our expert team provides quick and effective solutions to restore optimal function.

Tire Fitting & Alignment

Our tire fitting and alignment services ensure even wear, improved fuel efficiency, and better handling. Proper alignment extends tire life and ensures a smoother, safer ride.

Battery Services

Ensure your vehicle starts reliably every time with our battery services, including testing, replacement, charging and installation. We keep your electrical system in top condition for seamless performance.

Interior Cleaning

Revitalize your car’s interior with our thorough cleaning service. We expertly eliminate dirt, stains, and odors, leaving every surface—from carpets to upholstery—looking fresh, clean, and inviting.

Buffing & Polishing

Achieve a mirror-like finish with our buffing and polishing services. We remove imperfections, scratches, and swirl marks, enhancing your vehicle’s shine and providing a stunning, smooth surface.
